What is SIGN LANGUAGE TRANSLATOR?

SIGN LANGUAGE TRANSLATOR is an AI-powered system designed to make communication between deaf and hearing individuals simple and accessible. Since sign language is the primary communication method for many deaf users, interacting with people who do not understand sign language can be challenging.

Our system uses real-time webcam gesture recognition to convert sign language into English text instantly. Hearing users can read the translated message and respond through text, creating a smooth two-way conversation.

The platform also helps users learn sign language through tutorials and training videos while detecting incorrect gestures and helping improve signing accuracy.
